# Training Plan

## Plan Overview
- **Plan name**: Best Triathletes — Complete 24-Week Ironman 70.3
- **Duration**: 24 weeks
- **Start date**: May 11, 2026 (Monday)
- **Race date**: October 25, 2026 (Sunday)
- **Weekly hours**: 4.5–13.5 h (progressive)

## Training Zones

| Zone | Intensity | % Max HR | Feel |
|------|-----------|----------|------|
| Z1 | Very Easy | 50–60% | Effortless conversation, nasal breathing only |
| Z2 | Easy | 60–70% | Comfortable for hours, slight breathing increase |
| Z3 | Moderate | 70–80% | Comfortably hard, requires focus |
| Z4 | Hard | 80–90% | Hard effort, 3–8 min sustainability |
| Z5 | Very Hard | 90–100% | Maximum effort, 30 s – 2 min |

## Phases

| Phase | Weeks | Focus | Zone Mix |
|-------|-------|-------|----------|
| Base Building | 1–8 | Aerobic foundation, swim skill, strength patterns | 80% Z1-Z2, 20% Z3+ |
| Build | 9–16 | Volume increase, lactate threshold, VO2 max | 70% Z1-Z2, 30% Z3+ |
| Peak | 17–20 | Race simulation, sharpening | 60% Z1-Z2, 40% Z3+ |
| Taper | 21–24 | Reduce volume, maintain intensity, supercompensation | Volume ↓, intensity maintained |

## Strength Training Periodization

| Phase | Weeks | Reps | Load | Focus |
|-------|-------|------|------|-------|
| Anatomical Adaptation | 1–8 | 12–15 | Moderate | Movement patterns |
| Maximum Strength | 9–16 | 6–10 | Heavy | Power development |
| Power Endurance | 17–20 | Sport-specific | Explosive | Race-specific strength |
| Maintenance | 21–24 | Activation only | Light | No fatigue accumulation |

### Week 1 — Foundation Setting (Mon May 11 – Sun May 17) · 6.0 h

```
Mon: Swim — 1500m Z2
     Warm-up: 300m easy (200m free + 4×25m build)
     Main: 8×100m Z2 steady (15s rest)
     Cool-down: 300m easy choice
     Focus: Long strokes (16-18/25m), bilateral breathing every 3rd stroke, catch-up drill every 4th length

     Core — 15 min
     Planks: 3×30s standard + 2×10 single-arm reaches
     Side Planks: 2×20s each side + 10 hip dips
     Dead Bugs: 2×10 each side, 3s holds (30s rest)

Tue: Bike — 45 min Z2
     Warm-up: 10min easy + 3×1min builds
     Main: 30min steady Z2
     Cool-down: 5min easy
     Target: 85-95 RPM, smooth pedal stroke, relaxed shoulders. HR 60-70% max

     Strength — 30 min
     Goblet Squats: 3×12-15 (3s down, 1s pause, 2s up)
     Walking Lunges: 3×10 each leg
     Push-ups: 3×8-15 (progression based)
     Bent Rows: 3×12-15 (60-90s rest)

Wed: Run — 30 min Z2
     Warm-up: 10min easy + 4×15s strides
     Main: 15min steady Z2
     Cool-down: 5min walk/easy jog
     Focus: Midfoot landing, 180 steps/min cadence, relaxed arms. HR 60-70% max

     Flexibility — 15 min
     Hip Flexor Stretch: 2×30s each leg
     Hamstring Stretch: 2×30s each leg
     Calf Stretch: 2×30s each leg
     IT Band: 2×30s each side

Thu: Swim — 1800m Z2
     Warm-up: 400m easy (300m free + 4×25m drill)
     Main: 6×200m Z2 steady (30s rest)
     Cool-down: 200m easy
     Focus: Same stroke rate as Monday, practice sighting every 6 strokes

Fri: Rest — Complete physical and mental rest
     Light meal prep and hydration focus

Sat: Bike — 60 min Z2
     Warm-up: 10min easy + 3×1min builds
     Main: 45min steady aerobic
     Cool-down: 5min easy
     Practice nutrition every 20min

     Run — 15 min Z1-Z2 (Brick off bike)
     Focus on quick, light steps — heavy legs are normal

Sun: Run — 45 min Z2
     Warm-up: 10min easy
     Main: 30min steady Z2
     Cool-down: 5min walk
     Conversation pace throughout, nasal breathing preferred
```
