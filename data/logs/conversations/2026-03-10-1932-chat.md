### Summary
Athlete asked whether daily creatine supplementation would help. Coach explained the evidence base: strong support for strength/hypertrophy goals, modest direct benefit for aerobic performance, 1–2 kg water weight gain (not a concern given no time goal for Groningen or 70.3). Recommended 3–5g creatine monohydrate daily, starting at 3g given recent GI history.

### Key Points
- Athlete interested in creatine supplementation
- Coach recommendation: 3g/day monohydrate to start, no loading phase, any consistent timing
- Primary benefit: supports hypertrophy block and strength training capacity
- Secondary: minor benefit for high-intensity intervals within endurance training
- GI caution: start low given recent GI illness history
- Weight gain: 1–2 kg water retention, not a concern for athlete's current race goals

### Action Items
- Monitor GI tolerance in first 1–2 weeks at 3g/day
